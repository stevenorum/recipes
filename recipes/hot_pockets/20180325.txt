HOMEMADE CRUST

Ingredients

CRUST
-DRY
4 cups flour
1/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tbsp yeast
1 tbsp [basic italian seasoning](https://github.com/stevenorum/recipes/blob/master/recipes/italian%20seasoning/20180319.txt)
1 tsp [rosemary-heavy italias seasoning](https://github.com/stevenorum/recipes/blob/master/recipes/italian%20seasoning/20180319.txt)

-WET
1/4 cup olive oil
1 1/2 cups warm water


FILLINGS
2 1/2 cups of chopped fake meat
1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese
1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese
pizza sauce for pizza hot pockets

Instructions

1. Mix all the dry crust stuff.
2. Mix in the oil.
3. Mix in the water.
4. Cover and let rise until doubled in size.

5. Make 8-9" circles of dough.
6. Put stuff in 'em.
7. Fold in half and seal edges.
8. Bake at 450F for 12-15 minutes.
9. Freeze.

To reheat:
Nuke in a paper towel for ~90 seconds, or 120 if your microwave is as old and busted as mine.
